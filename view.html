Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler and Dave Krinsky, that premiered on April 6, 2014, on HBO. The series focuses on five young men who founded a startup company in Silicon Valley. A sixth and final season consisting of seven episodes premiered on October 27, 2019, and concluded on December 8.
